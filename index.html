Who am I?

I'm Randy and I'm a junior software engineer. A native of Southern California, I moved to Brooklyn in August 2017 where I've been enjoying life ever since. Aside from software engineering, I like to pursue my hobbies (of which there may be too many). Some of my hobbies include listening to music, playing music, reading up on history and science, and traveling.

As of mid-February 2018, I just graduated from Fullstack Academy of Code in NYC where I learned the ins-and-outs of contemporary web single page application design with a stack consisting of Node, React, Redux, Express, and Postgres.

What can I do?

Some of the languages I am proficient/knowledgeable in include C, C++ (11 and 14), Java, JavaScript (ES6 and TypeScript), and Python. With Node.js, some of the frameworks I enjoy using are React, Redux, Express, Koa, and Sequelize.

I like to make applications that don't beat you over the head with intense information overload. I'm mostly interested in providing a clean, soft user experience.

About this site...

This site was developed entirely with basic HTML and CSS and deployed with Google App Engine. After graduating from Fullstack Academy, I realized that I hadn't constructed a simple webpage for fun ever since my foray into Node.js -- the last time might've been when Geocities was still around. So I thought to myself, why not?

Oh, and feel free to contact me through any of the links in the footer!
